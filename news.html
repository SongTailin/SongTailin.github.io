[NEWS023-002] First Scientific Research Project Led by Tailin Song Approved Successfully (2023.06.08)

[NEWS023-002] 宋泰霖主持的首个科研项目成功立项 (2023.06.08)

The undergraduate research training and innovation entrepreneurship project "Individual Identification and Posture Analysis of Wild Birds Based on Deep Learning" Led by Tailin Song was successfully approved in the School of Artificial Intelligence of Beijing Normal University at the university level.

This project approval marks the last step of Tailin Song's successful completion of the core goal of this stage, which is to achieve comprehensive victory.

Thanks very much to Beijing Normal University, Key Laboratory of Conservation Ecology of Northeast Tiger and Leopard National Park, School of Artificial Intelligence, and College of Life Sciences for their support to this project. Thanks very much to all the mentors, senior brothers and sisters for their guidance and assistance to this project.

宋泰霖申报的本科生科研训练与创新创业项目"基于深度学习的野生鸟类个体识别与姿态分析"在北京师范大学人工智能学院成功立项，立项级别为校级。

此次立项，标志着宋泰霖成功完成本阶段的核心目标"夺取全面胜利"诸多任务的最后一环，成功实现了夺取全面胜利。
