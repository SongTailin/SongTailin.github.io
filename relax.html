Clap 刺儿

"Clap" is a multiplayer real-time hand game initiated by Xiaotian Dou and collectively created, revised, and developed by Class 6 of 2018 of Yanji Sixth Middle School. It has the characteristics of short game time, clear rules, multiple changes, and strong fun. I am currently writing a rulebook for the basic rules and various gameplay modes of "Clap". Stay tuned!

“刺儿”(cier1)是由豆校田发起的、延吉市第六中学18届6班集体创作修订发扬的多人即时手技游戏，具有游戏时长短、规则明确、变化多、趣味性强的特点。我正在编写“刺儿”的基础规则和多种玩法的规则书，敬请期待！
