Clap 呲儿

"Clap" is a multiplayer real-time hand game initiated by Xiaotian Dou and collectively created, revised, and developed by Class 6 of 2018 of Yanji No.6 Middle School. It has the characteristics of short game time, clear rules, multiple changes, and strong fun. Sorry, there is currently no English version of the rulebook for "Clap".

“呲儿”(cier1)是由豆校田发起的、延吉市第六中学18届6班集体创作修订发扬的多人即时手技游戏，具有游戏时长短、规则明确、变化多、趣味性强的特点。我编写的《呲儿》规则书已经发布，始终欢迎玩家们的改进、创新和二次创作。
